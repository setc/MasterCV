%	What is this?

As everyone know, sending generic not-tuned curriculums is the surepath to not
to be read. But, making a curriculum suited to each job offer may be a lot of
work.

So I decided to create a megacurriculum to everything and the kitchen sink as a 
base to create curriculums ready to send. I just need to comment the parts I
don't need and voila, a new curriculum is born. Of course I can add and rewrite,
but in the master version I can keep everything just in case.
